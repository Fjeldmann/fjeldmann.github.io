    <h1 class="legal-heading">How we handle your data</h1>

    <h2>Data controller</h2>
    <p>
      Fjeldmann Solutions ApS<br>
      Registration no. (CVR) 44 81 24 37<br>
      <a href="mailto:contact@fjeldmann.dk">contact@fjeldmann.dk</a>
    </p>

    <h2>What we receive</h2>
    <p>
      When you submit the contact form on fjeldmann.dk, you send us:
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li>Your name</li>
      <li>Your e-mail address</li>
      <li>The message you write</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>FormSubmit as data processor</h2>
    <p>
      Form submissions are handled by <a href="https://formsubmit.co" target="_blank" rel="noopener">FormSubmit</a>,
      a third-party service that receives the form data and forwards it as an e-mail to our inbox at
      <a href="mailto:contact@fjeldmann.dk">contact@fjeldmann.dk</a>.
    </p>
    <p>
      FormSubmit states that it does not collect personally identifiable information beyond what&rsquo;s needed
      to deliver the service, and that we &mdash; as the recipient &mdash; remain the rightful owner of the
      information you send through the form. FormSubmit may use cookies as part of delivering the service
      and for security. You can read their full
      <a href="https://formsubmit.co/privacy.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">privacy policy</a> for the details.
    </p>

    <h2>How we use your message</h2>
    <p>
      We use your information only to reply to your enquiry and for any follow-up conversation. We don&rsquo;t share
      your data with third parties, and we don&rsquo;t use it for marketing.
    </p>

    <h2>How long we keep it</h2>
    <p>
      Messages are retained in our e-mail for as long as the conversation is relevant &mdash; typically no longer
      than 24 months. After that we delete them, unless we&rsquo;re required to keep them for accounting or
      contractual reasons.
    </p>

    <h2>Cookies</h2>
    <p>
      fjeldmann.dk does not use cookies for analytics, marketing, or tracking. We collect no information about
      your activity on the site, which is why you don&rsquo;t see a cookie banner.
    </p>
    <p>
      If you submit the contact form, FormSubmit may set strictly necessary cookies as part of its captcha and
      security checks. These cookies are used only to filter spam and protect the service. See FormSubmit&rsquo;s
      <a href="https://formsubmit.co/privacy.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">privacy policy</a> for details.
    </p>
    <p>
      You can clear cookies at any time through your browser. Doing so won&rsquo;t affect your ability to contact us.
    </p>

    <h2>Your rights</h2>
    <p>
      Under the GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data &mdash;
      and to lodge a complaint with the Danish Data Protection Agency (Datatilsynet) if you believe we&rsquo;re
      handling something incorrectly.
    </p>
    <p>
      Write to <a href="mailto:contact@fjeldmann.dk">contact@fjeldmann.dk</a> and we&rsquo;ll reply within one business day.
    </p>
